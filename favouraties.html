Palak Paneer

A creamy spinach and cottage cheese curry.

Category: Indian

How to Make

  1. Blanch spinach and puree it.
  2. Sauté onions, tomatoes, and spices.
  3. Add spinach puree and paneer cubes.
  4. Cook until the curry is creamy, and serve hot.
